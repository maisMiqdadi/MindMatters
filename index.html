Common Charges

"Common charges" typically refers to fees or expenses that are shared among multiple parties within a shared space, such as a residential building, commercial property, or community. These charges are often associated with the maintenance, operation, and management of the shared facilities or common areas

Real life cases

Real-life cases, also known as real-world cases or practical cases, refer to situations, examples, or scenarios drawn from actual events or experiences that illustrate concepts, principles, or theories in a particular field of study or practice. In various disciplines, including law, medicine, education, business, and engineering, real-life cases are used to provide context, application, and relevance to theoretical knowledge.
